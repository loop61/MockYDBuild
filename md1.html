Meet the Teacher:

When Justin saw Toy Story for the first time as a kid, he knew he wanted to work with technology when he grew up. Since then, he’s been creating, teaching, and working on his Enderdragon impressions. He took everything he learned as a kid, and working with kids, and turned it into Youth Digital. Now you can learn how to code with Youth Digital’s founder.


​In Mod Design 1 Kids Learn:​

Foundations of Java™

Kids learn Java, the most widely-used programming language in the world, including instantiation, methods, parameters, loops and more.

Industry-standard Coding Skills

​Kids learn how to mod like the pros as they sort through complex source code, edit and fine-tune attribute values, properly comment their code, and fix bugs as they arise.

Eclipse, a Professional Software

​Kids learn to use Eclipse, the same Java™ Development kit used by professionals to create Minecraft® and many other commercial programs.

How to make a real Mod

Kids learn how to create a real mod using the same tools and techniques as the most well-known modders. At the end of the course, they can easily share their mod with friends!


Mod Design 1 Requires:

  • Windows 10, 8,7, or Vista, or Mac: OS 10.8 or higher, 64-bit (after 2008)
  • Chrome, Firefox or Safari web browser
  • Network speeds greater than 1mbps
  • Minecraft® for Mac or PC (not pocket edition, or Windows 10 edition) on your computer. Mod Design 1 reverts to Minecraft® version 1.6.4 for testing. Upon completion, the mods can be easily exported to 1.11
  • Computers must meet the hardware requirements of Minecraft®.
