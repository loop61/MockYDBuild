﻿Code your own 3D Game with Unity®

Learn the fundamentals of Coding in C# and Game Development in Unity® as you create your own video game.


Meet the Teacher:

Sean is a designer with expertise in all things 3D! From Game Development to 3D Modeling and Animation, he loves making art come to life with amazing technology. Sean has even won a couple of national short film contests, but as you’ll see, he still doesn’t take himself too seriously.​​


In 3D Game Development 1 ​Kids Learn:​

Coding and Design Concepts

Kids learn not only the most important concepts in programming such as conditional statements, variables, methods, parameters, and loops, but they also learn essential design principles as they lay out and design their 3D Environment.

Industry-standard Game Development Workflows

​Kids learn the same fundamentals that are utilized in real Game Studios around the world including the use of prefabs, debugging, waypoints, particle systems, randomization, and more..

Unity®, a Professional Software

​​Kids learn how to use Unity®, the same professional software used to create Temple Run®, Pokémon Go®, and Myst®. It is one of the most powerful and widely-used Game Engines in the world and can export to iOS, Android, PC, Mac, XBox, PS4, Oculus, Cardboard, and Web.*

*The game made during the course will be compatible with PC and Mac.

How to make a real Game

Kids learn how to make a video game that looks like the ones they’d want to play. They also have creative control over the look and feel of the game and will be so excited to share what they’ve created with family and friends!
